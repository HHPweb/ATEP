Athletic Training Alumni Scholarship

The Athletic Training Alumni Scholarship is awarded annually to a deserving undergraduate Athletic Training student. The scholarship is supported entirely by donations. Gifts are tax-deductible and can be accomplished by contacting gift officer, Mr. Don Leggett at 252-328-9581 or by e-mail to leggettd@ecu.edu.

Athletic Training Alumni Group

Contributions can be sent to Tammy Garris, Office of Gift Records, Greenville Centre, Room 2212, 2200 South Charles Blvd, Greenville, NC, 27858. Checks can be made to Athletic Trainers Alumni Fund. Gifts are tax-deductible.

Athletic Training Student Club

All ECU Athletic Training Student Club monies are raised via numerous fundraising events throughout the year. To donate to the ATS Club, contact Club Advisor, Andrew Pickett, at 252-737-1476, or at picketta@ecu.edu.
